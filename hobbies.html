My pastime: Football (Soccer)

I enjoy watching the Vancouver Whitecaps® play. Recently, they signed the German soccer legend Thomas Müller, who scored a hat-trick in his last game. His first two goals came from PKs (penalty kicks), while his third was a header assisted by Giuseppe Bovalina, making the score 7–0 against Philadelphia on his birthday this past Saturday at BC Place.

Happy 36th birthday, Thomas Müller!
